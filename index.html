IQ invoice - Automation System !
IQ Invoice is a user-friendly routing system designed to mirror your current approval processes and workflows, but without all of the paper and inefficiencies.

This means the invoice will end up in the right place at the right time. Make use of the system’s high-level reporting function. Tracking can be done through the entire lifecycle of the invoice and each stage of the invoice can be identified easily. If an invoice is in the system it is available no matter where you are. With 24/7 access, you can view or approve everything in real-time.

Features

Visibility and control

You have all invoice information at your fingertips: supplier data, amount to be paid, taxes, line item details, workflow status, audit trail and invoice images / PDF.

Improved bottom line

Early payment discounts, in combination with the elimination of late payment penalties, double payments and fraudulent invoices—make a substantial contribution to the improvement of your bottom line.

Early payment discounts

With the automated approval of most purchase order invoices and a smooth workflow for the rest, you often can negotiate a number of favorable payment terms and price lists.

Cash flow management

Visibility and control, along with timely and accurate reports, enable healthy cash flow decisions. You avoid late payment penalties while taking advantage of payment discounts.

Financial reports

You have immediate access to every kind of statistic you require, in charts, graphs and tables, such as discount reports, production volume reports and lead-time reports

No late-payment penalties

An automated accounts payable workflow typically reduces the invoice cycle time by 50 to 80%, which gives you ample time to work within payment terms.
